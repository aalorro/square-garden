Privacy Policy

Square Garden — Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Square Garden ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by ArtMondo. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Square Garden operates entirely offline and does not transmit any data from your device to external servers.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or gameplay statistics
  • Photos, files, or any other media from your device

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide game functionality:

  • Your in-game profile (username, avatar selection, preferences)
  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)
  • App settings (sound, theme preferences)

This data is stored using Android's local DataStore and never leaves your device. You can clear this data at any time through the App's Settings menu or by uninstalling the App.

Internet Access

Square Garden does not require an internet connection and does not access the internet for any purpose. The App contains no network-related code, APIs, or server communications.

Third-Party Services

Square Garden does not integrate with any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting tools

Children's Privacy

Square Garden is designed to be safe for players of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data whatsoever, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. No account creation, email address, or personal details are ever required to use the App.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. There is nothing to share, sell, or transfer.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from our servers. Your game data is protected by your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
